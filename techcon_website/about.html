Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for developers and has grown into a global conference attracting thousands of attendees annually.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to empower the global tech community by fostering knowledge exchange, celebrating innovation, and inspiring the next generation of leaders.

Notable Past Speakers

  • Ada Lovelace – Pioneer in computing theory and STEM education.
  • Alan Turing – Innovator in artificial intelligence and cryptography.
  • Grace Hopper – Advocate for women in tech and programming languages.
